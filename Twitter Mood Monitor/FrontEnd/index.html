Twitter Mood Monitor is an application designed to help you visualize the mood of cities in the United States. When you select a region, you will be directed to our map of live moods. The mood is determined by reading recent Tweets. More :) make for a happy city, and more :( means the city is having a bad day.

Select a region above to begin! You will be redirected to our map page.
